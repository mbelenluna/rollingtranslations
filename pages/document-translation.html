Document Translation

Our document translation service goes beyond mere language conversion. We understand the importance of effectively reaching your target audience, which is why we meticulously select linguists from the specific city or country of your intended readers. This ensures that your content is not only accurately translated, but also culturally adapted to resonate with the local audience.

Specialized Translation

We understand the importance of domain-specific terminology and expertise. Our team of specialized translators, versed in areas such as legal, medical, financial, and more, delivers industry-specific translations that meet the highest standards of accuracy and confidentiality. We create and maintain glossaries and termbases for each field of expertise and for each different client. This ensures consistency throughout all the projects completed with each specific client.

Our Workflow

Desktop Publishing

Does your document have a complex layout that only a professional graphic designer would be able to handle professionaly? We have a team of expert desktop publishing artists that works closely with our translation team to ensure that the layout, formatting, and design elements seamlessly integrate translated text, maintaining consistency and professionalism across your materials.

Our professional DTP artists can handle a variety of graphic design programs, such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Sketch, Canva, etc. If you are unsure if we handle your file extension, feel free to drop us a message in the form below and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

We also offer Document Typing services, which means our DTP artists team recreate complex documents such as scanned medical records with utmost accuracy, including retyping the text while maintaing the exact same layout as the original document.
