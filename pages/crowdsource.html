FAQ

Is crowdsourced translation right for me?

Yes, if you need fast, affordable human translations for non‑regulated, high‑volume content where consistency and brand nuance are important but not mission‑critical.

What content is not allowed?

We do not process medical, legal, government/regulated, or highly confidential materials in the crowdsourced workflow. Please use our Professional Services.

How do you ensure quality?

Vetted linguists, peer review for short texts, segment‑level QA for bulk work, and optional editor passes. We can also layer AI‑assisted checks under human oversight.

Is my data safe?

Crowdsourcing involves multiple contributors; for sensitive content we recommend Professional Services with NDAs, limited access, and single‑owner workflows.
