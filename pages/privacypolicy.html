Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2/26/2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when visitors fill out the form on our website:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (if provided)
  • Project Details (source and target languages) (if provided)

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information solely to provide accurate quotes for the specified translation projects.
In addition to the uses described above, we also use the information collected from newsletter subscribers (name and email address) for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the requested free guide upon registration.
  • To send periodic newsletters containing company updates, industry news, and promotional offers (only if the user opts-in to receive promotions).

3. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share any personal information with third parties.

4. Consent

By submitting the form, visitors are consenting to us using their information to provide a quote for their project.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data for 3 years from the moment we receive the form submission. After this period, data will be disposed of unless the project is still ongoing or there are ongoing matters related to the project, such as an unpaid invoice in connection with said project.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to, and request portability of their data. To exercise these rights, users can contact the company at info@rolling-translations.com.

7. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data through the following measures:

  • Encryption Standards: We employ industry-standard encryption for the secure transfer of files containing sensitive information.
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs): All employees, including translators, are required to sign NDAs to safeguard the confidentiality of client information.
  • Security Awareness Training: Our team undergoes regular security awareness training to uphold data protection standards.
  • Vulnerability Scans: We conduct regular system scans to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

8. Updates to the Policy

The privacy policy is always available on the website, and users can check the last update date. If a user has submitted the form, they will receive a notification in their email when the policy is updated.

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, users can contact Rolling Translations at info@rolling-translations.com.
